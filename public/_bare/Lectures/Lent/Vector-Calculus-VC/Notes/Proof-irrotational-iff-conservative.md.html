Suppose $$ F_i = \pdfd\phi{x^i}. $$

Then $$ \nabla\times\v F = \epsilon_{ijk}\v e_k \pdfd{F_j}{x_i} = \epsilon_{ijk}\v e_k\frac{\partial^2\phi}{\partial x^i\partial x^j} = 0$$ by anti-symmetry.

Conversely, apply Stokes' theorem: $$ \nabla\times\v F=\v 0 \implies \oint_C \v F\cdot\,d\v x = 0 \implies \v F=\nabla\phi. $$
