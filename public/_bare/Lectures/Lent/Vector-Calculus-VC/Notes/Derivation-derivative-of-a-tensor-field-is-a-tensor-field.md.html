Under a change of basis $x'_i = R_{ik} x_k$, we have $x_k = R_{ik} x_i'$, so $$ \begin{align*} \pdd{x_i'} = \pdfd{x_k}{x_i'} \pdd{x_k} = R_{ik} \pdd{x_k} \end{align*} $$ so $$ \begin{align*} X_{i_1 \ldots i_q j_1 \ldots j_p}' (\v x) &= \pdd{x_{i_1'}} \ldots \pdd{x_{i_q}} T'_{j_1\ldots j_p}(\v x) \\ &= R_{i_1 k_1}\ldots R_{i_q k_q} \pdd{x_{k_1}} \ldots \pdd{x_{k_q}} R_{j_1 l_1} \ldots R_{j_p l_p} T_{l_1\ldots l_p}(\v x) \\ &= R_{i_1 k_1} \ldots R_{i_p k_p} R_{j_1 l_1} \ldots R_{j_p l_p} X_{k_1\ldots k_q l_1 \ldots j_p}(\v x). \end{align*} $$
