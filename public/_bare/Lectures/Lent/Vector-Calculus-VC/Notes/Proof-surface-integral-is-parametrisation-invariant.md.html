Let $\v x(u, v)$ and $\v x(\tilde u(u, v), \tilde v(u,v))$ be two parametrisations. Then by chain rule, $$ \begin{align*} \pdvd x{\tilde u} &= \pdfd u{\tilde u} \pdvd x u + \pdfd v{\tilde u} \pdvd x v, \\ \pdvd x{\tilde v} &= \pdfd u{\tilde v} \pdvd x u + \pdfd v{\tilde v} \pdvd x v. \\ \end{align*} $$ So $$ \pdvd x{\tilde u}\times \pdvd x{\tilde v} = \bigg(\pdfd u {\tilde u}\pdfd v {\tilde v} - \pdfd v{\tilde u} \pdfd u{\tilde v}\bigg) \pdvd xu\times \pdvd xv = \pdfd{(u, v)}{(\tilde u, \tilde v)}\ \pdvd xu\times\pdvd xv.$$ Hence for scalar field, using change of variable, $$ \begin{align*} \int_S \phi\, dS &= \int_D \phi(\v x(u, v))\, \bigg|\pdvd x u\times\pdvd x v\bigg| \,du\,dv \\ &= \int_{\tilde D} \phi(\v x(\tilde u, \tilde v))\, \bigg|\pdvd x u\times\pdvd x v\bigg|\,\bigg|\pdfd{(u,v)}{(\tilde u,\tilde v)}\bigg| \,d\tilde u\,d\tilde v \\ &= \int_{\tilde D} \phi(\v x(\tilde u, \tilde v))\,\bigg|\pdvd x{\tilde u}\times \pdvd x{\tilde v}\bigg|\, d\tilde u\, d\tilde v \end{align*} $$ as required. For a vector field, $$\begin{align*} \int_S \v F\cdot d\v S &= \int_D \v F\big(\v x(u,v)\big) \cdot \Big(\pdvd xu \times \pdvd xv\Big)\,du\,dv \\ &= \int_{\tilde D} \v F(\v x(\tilde u,\tilde v)\big)\cdot\Big(\pdvd xu \times \pdvd xv\Big)\bigg|\pdfd{(u,v)}{(\tilde u,\tilde v)}\bigg|\,d\tilde u\, d\tilde v \\ &= \pm\int_{\tilde D} \v F\big(\v x(\tilde u,\tilde v)\big) \cdot \Big(\pdvd x{\tilde u} \times \pdvd x{\tilde v}\Big)\,d\tilde u\,d\tilde v \\ \end{align*}$$
