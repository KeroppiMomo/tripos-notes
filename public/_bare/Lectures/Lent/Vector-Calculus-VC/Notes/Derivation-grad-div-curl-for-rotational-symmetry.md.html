We have $$ r^2 = x^i x^i \implies 2r\pdfd r{x^i} = 2x^i \implies \pdfd r{x^i} = \frac{x^i}{r} $$ so $$\begin{align*} \nabla r^p &= \v e_i \pdfd{r^p}{x^i} \\ &= pr^{p-1} \pdfd r{x^i} \v e_i \\ &= pr^{p-1} \vhat r. \end{align*}$$

For divergence, $$ \nabla\cdot\v r = \pdfd{x^i}{x^i} = \delta_{ii} = n $$ and for curl, $$ \nabla\times\v r = \epsilon_{ijk} \pdfd{x^j}{x^i} \v e_k = \v 0 $$ by anti-symmetry.
