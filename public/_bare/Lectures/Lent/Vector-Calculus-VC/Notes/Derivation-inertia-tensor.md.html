A particle at $\v x_a$ rotates as $$ \begin{align*} \dv x_a = \v \omega\times \v x_a. \end{align*} $$ Angular momentum by definition is $$ \begin{align*} \v L &= \sum_a m_a \v x_a\times \dv x_a \\ &= \sum_a m_a \v x_a\times(\v \omega\times\v x_a) \\ &= \sum_a m_a\left(|\v x_a|^2\v \omega - (\v x_a\cdot\v \omega)\v x_a\right) \\ L_i &= \left(\sum_a m_a\left(|\v x_a|^2\delta_{ij} - (\v x_a)_i (\v x_a)_j\right)\right)\omega_j. \end{align*} $$
