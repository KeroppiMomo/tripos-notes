For gradient, using chain rule, $$\begin{align*} df &= \nabla f\cdot d\v x \\ \pdfd fu\, du + \pdfd fv\, dv + \pdfd fw\,dw &=\nabla f\cdot(h_u\v e_u\,du + h_v\v e_v\,dv + h_w\v e_w\, dw) \\ \end{align*}$$ so by orthonormality of $\v e_u, \v e_v, \v e_w$, $$ \nabla f = \frac1{h_u}\pdfd fu\v e_u + \frac1{h_v} \pdfd fv\v e_v + \frac{1}{h_w}\pdfd fw \v e_w. $$
