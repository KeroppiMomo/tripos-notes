There are two linearly independent vectors $\v m_1$ and $\v m_2$ that lie tangent to the surface. Since $\v m_i\cdot\nabla F$ is the rate of change of $F(\v x)$ in the direction of $\v m_i$, $$ m_i \cdot \nabla F = 0. $$

Therefore since the normal vector $\v n$ is perpendicular to both $\v m_1$ and $\v m_2$, $\v n$ is parallel to $\nabla F$.
