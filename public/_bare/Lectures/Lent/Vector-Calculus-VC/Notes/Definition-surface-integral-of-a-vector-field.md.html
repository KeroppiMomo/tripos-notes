For a surface $S$ parametrised by $\v x(u, v)$ and a vector field $\v F: \R^3\to\R^3$, the surface integral of $\v F$ over $S$ is $$ \int_S \v F(\v x)\cdot d\v S = \int_D \Big(\pdvd xu\times\pdvd xv\Big)\cdot \v F\big(\v x(u,v)\big) \,du\,dv$$ where $d\v S = \v n\, dS$ is the area element. The integral is also called the flux of $\v F$ through the surface $S$.
