The anti-symmetric piece is $$ \begin{align*} A_{ij} &= \frac{1}{2}\left(\pdfd{F_i}{x^j} - \pdfd{F_j}{x_i}\right) \\ &= \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{ijk}\epsilon_{pqk} \pdfd{F_p}{x^q} \\ &=-\frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{ijk}\left(\nabla\times\v F\right)_k. \end{align*} $$

The symmetric piece is $$ \begin{align*} S_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\pdfd{F_i}{x^j} + \pdfd{F_j}{x^i}\right). \end{align*} $$

Trace is $$ \begin{align*} Q = \pdfd{F_i}{x^i} = \nabla\cdot\v F, \end{align*} $$ so the traceless symmetric piece is $$ \begin{align*} P_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\pdfd{F_i}{x^j} + \pdfd{F_j}{x^i}\right) - \frac{1}{3}(\nabla\cdot\v F)\delta_{ij}. \end{align*} $$
