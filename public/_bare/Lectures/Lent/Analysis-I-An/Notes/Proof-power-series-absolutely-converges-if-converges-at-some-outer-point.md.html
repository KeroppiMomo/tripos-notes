$z=0$ is trivial.

Since $\sum a_n z^n$ converges, $a_n z^n\to0$ so it is bounded, i.e. there exists $K>0$ such that $|a_n z_n|\leq K$ for all $n$.

Now $$ |a_nw^n| = |a_n z^n|\left|\frac wz\right|^n \leq Kp^n $$ where $p=|w/z|<1$. Hence $\sum |a_n w^n|$ converges by comparison to the geometric series $\sum Kp^n$ which converges.
