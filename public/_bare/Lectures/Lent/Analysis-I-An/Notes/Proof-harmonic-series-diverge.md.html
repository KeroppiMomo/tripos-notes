Let $S_n = \fsum j1n \frac1j$. Then $$\begin{align*} S_{2n} &= S_n + \frac1{n+1} + \frac1{n+2} + \ldots + \frac1{2n} \\ &\geq S_n + \frac1{2n} + \frac1{2n} + \ldots + \frac1{2n} \\ S_{2n} - S_n &\geq \frac12. \end{align*}$$

So if $S_n \to S$, then $S_{2n} - S_n \to S-S = 0<1/2$, which is impossible since $1/2$ is a lower bound of the sequence $S_{2n} - S_n$. Hence $S_n$ diverges.
