Consider a discrete random variable $X$ taking the values $x_1,\ldots,x_n$ with equal probability $1/n$.

Apply Jensen's inequality to the convex function $-\log x$, $$ \begin{align*} -\log\bigg(\frac1n\fsum k1n x_k\bigg) &\leq -\frac1n\fsum k1n \log x_k \\ &= -\log\bigg(\for\prod k1n x_k\bigg)^{1/n} \end{align*}$$ so $$ \bigg(\for\prod k1n x_k\bigg)^{1/n} \leq \frac1n\fsum k1n x_k. $$
