$$ \begin{align*} \prob(Z\leq z) &= \int_{\R^2} 1_{\set{x+y\leq z}} f_X(x) f_Y(y) \,dx\,dy \\ &= \int_{\R} \int_{-\infty}^{z-y} f_X(x) f_Y(y) \, dx \, dy \\ &= \int_{\R} \int_{-\infty}^{z} f_X(w-y) f_Y(y) \, dw \, dy && w=x+y\\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{z} \int_{\R} f_X(w-y) f_Y(y) \, dy \, dw && \text{Fubini's theorem} \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{z} f_X * f_Y(w)\, dw \end{align*} $$ so $$ \begin{align*} f_Z = f_X * f_Y. \end{align*} $$
