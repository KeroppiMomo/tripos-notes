Let $X\sim E(\lambda)$ and set $X_n = \lfloor nX \rfloor$. $X_n$ takes values in $\Z^+$ and $$ \begin{align*} \prob(X_n\geq k) = \prob(nX \geq k) = e^{-\lambda k/n}. \end{align*} $$ So $X_n$ is a geometric random variable of parameter $p_n = 1-e^{-\lambda/n}$.

As $n\to\infty$, $$ \begin{align*} p_n \sim \frac{\lambda}{n}, \quad \frac{X_n}{n} \to X. \end{align*} $$
