$$ \begin{align*} M_X(t) &= \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{tx} \frac{\lambda^\alpha x^{\alpha-1} e^{-\lambda x}}{\Gamma(a)} \, dx \\ &= \left(\frac{\lambda}{\lambda-t}\right)^\alpha \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{(\lambda-t)^\alpha x^{\alpha-1} e^{-(\lambda-t)x}}{\Gamma(a)}\, dx \\ &= \begin{cases} \left(\dfrac{\lambda}{\lambda-t}\right)^\alpha, &t<\lambda, \\ \infty, &t\geq\lambda. \end{cases} \end{align*} $$
