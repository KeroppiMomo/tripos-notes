Density function of $R$ is $$ \begin{align*} f_R(r) = f_V(v) \left|\dfd vr \right| = re^{-r^2/2}, \quad r\in [0, \infty). \end{align*} $$ Then the joint density function of $X,Y$ are $$ \begin{align*} f_{X,Y}(x,y) = f_{R,\Theta}(r,\theta) \left|\pdfd{(x,y)}{(r,\theta)}\right|\inv &= re^{-r^2/2}\cdot 1 \cdot \frac{1}{r} = e^{-x^2/2} \cdot e^{-y^2/2} \end{align*} $$ so $X,Y$ are independent $N(0,1)$ random variables.
