For $X = (X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n)$, the expectation is $$ \begin{align*} \expect(u^T X) &\equiv \big(\expect(u_1 X_1), \expect(u_2 X_2), \ldots, \expect(u_n X_n)\big) \\ &= u^T \expect(X) &&\text{linearity}\\ &= u^T \mu. \end{align*} $$ The variance is $$ \begin{align*} \op{var}(u^T X) &= \expect((u^T X - u^T\mu)^2) \\ &= \expect(u^T(X-\mu)(X-\mu)^T u) \\ &= u^T \expect((X-\mu)(X-\mu)^T) u &&\text{linearity} \\ &= u^T V u. \end{align*} $$
