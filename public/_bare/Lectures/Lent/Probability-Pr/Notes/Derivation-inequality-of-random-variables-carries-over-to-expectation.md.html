We have $$ Y = X + (Y-X)1_{\set{X<\infty}} $$ where $(Y-X)1_{\set{X<\infty}}$ is a non-negative random variable. (The $1_{\set{X<\infty}}$ is to ensure $\infty-\infty$ doesn't cause problem when $X=Y=\infty$.)

Hence taking expectation, $$ \expect(Y) = \expect(X) + \expect\big((Y-X)1_{\set{X<\infty}}\big) \geq \expect(X). $$
