$$\begin{align*} G_{X+Y}(t) &= \expect(t^{X+Y}) \\ &= \expect(t^Xt^Y) \\ &= \expect(t^X)\expect(t^Y) &&\text{independence} \\ &= G_X(t)\, G_Y(t). \end{align*}$$

Alternatively, $$\begin{align*} G_{X+Y}(t) &= \fsum n0\infty \prob(X+Y=n)\,t^n \\ &= \fsum n0\infty \fsum k0n \prob(X=k)\prob(Y=n-k)\,t^n &&\text{independence} \\ &= \fsum k0\infty\prob(X=k)\,t^k \fsum nk\infty\prob(Y=n-k)\,t^{n-k} &&\text{non-negative terms} \\ &= G_X(t) G_Y(t). \end{align*}$$
