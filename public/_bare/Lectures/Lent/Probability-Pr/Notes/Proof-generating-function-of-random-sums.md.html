First we condition on specific values of $N$: $$\begin{align*} \expect(t^{S_N}|N=k) &= \expect(t^{S_k}) \\ &= \expect(t^{X_1}\times\ldots t^{X_k}) \\ &= \expect(t^{X_1})\times\ldots\times\expect(t^{X_k}) \\ &= G_X(t)^k. \end{align*}$$ So using the law of total expectation, $$\begin{align*} G_{S_N}(t) &= \expect(t^{S_n}) \\ &= \fsum k0\infty \prob(N=k)\,G_X(t)^k \\ &= G_N(G_X(t)). \end{align*}$$
