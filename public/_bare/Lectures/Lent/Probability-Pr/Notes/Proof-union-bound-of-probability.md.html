Set $$ B_1 = A_1, \quad B_n = A_n \setminus (A_1\union A_2\union\ldots\union A_{n-1}). $$ The sequence $(B_n)_{n\in\N}$ is a sequence of disjoint sets in $\mathcal F$, $\mathbb P(B_n)\leq \mathbb P(A_n)$ since $B_n\subseteq A_n$, and $\bigcup_n B_n = \bigcup_n A_n$.

Hence $$ \mathbb P\Big(\bigcup_n A_n\Big) = \mathbb P\Big(\bigcup_n B_n\Big) = \sum_n \mathbb P(B_n) \leq \sum_n \mathbb P(A_n). $$
