The case when $\lambda=0$ is clear.

For $\lambda>0$, $$\lambda X = (\lambda X)^+ - (\lambda X)^- = \lambda X^+ - \lambda X^-$$ so $$\lambda X + \lambda X^- = \lambda X.$$ Taking expectation on both sides, $$ \expect(\lambda X) + \lambda\expect(X^-) = \lambda\expect(X^+)$$ and rearrange to get the result.

For $\lambda<0$, $$ \lambda X = (-|\lambda| X)^+ - (-|\lambda| X)^- = |\lambda|(X^- - X^+) = \lambda(X^+ - X^-)$$ and proceed as above.
