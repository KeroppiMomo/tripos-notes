Note the inequality $$ (k\sigma)^2\, 1_{|X-\mu|\geq k\sigma} \leq (X-\mu)^2.$$ To see this, when $|X-\mu|<k\sigma$, LHS = 0, and when $|X-\mu|\geq k\sigma$, square both sides to see the inequality.

Taking expectation, $$ (k\sigma)^2\, \prob(|X-\mu|\geq k\sigma) \leq \expect((X-\mu)^2) = \sigma^2, $$ then rearrange.
