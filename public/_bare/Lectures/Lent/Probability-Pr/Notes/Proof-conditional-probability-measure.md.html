We'll check the two properties of a probability measure:

  • $\mathbb P_B(\Omega) = \mathbb P(B)/\mathbb P(B) = 1$.

  • For any sequence $(A_n)_{n\in\N}$ of disjoint sets in $\mathcal F$, $$\begin{align*} \mathbb P_B\Big(\for\bigcup n1\infty A_n\Big) &= \frac{\mathbb P\big((\for\bigcup n1\infty A_n)\cap B\big)}{\mathbb P(B)} \\ &= \frac{\prob\big(\for\bigcup n1\infty (A_n\cap B)\big)}{\mathbb P(B)} \\ &= \frac{\fsum n1\infty \prob(A_n\cap B)}{\prob(B)} \\ &= \fsum n1\infty \prob_B(A_n). \end{align*}$$
