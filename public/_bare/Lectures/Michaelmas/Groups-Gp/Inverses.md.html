Notation (inverse) open_in_new

The inverse of an element $a\in G$ is denoted $a^{-1}$.

This is due to the uniqueness of inverses.

Proposition (inverse of an inverse) open_in_new

For any $a\in G$, $$(a^{-1})^{-1} = a.$$

Proof

Proposition (inverse of a product) open_in_new

For any $a,b\in G$, $$ (a\cdot b)^{-1} = b^{-1}\cdot a^{-1}. $$

Proof
