By this, composition of Mobius transformations is also a Mobius transformation.

Composition of functions is associative.

$\mrm{Id}: z\mapsto z$ is clearly an identity.

If $\mu(z) = \frac{az+b}{cz+d}$, let $$ \nu(z) = \frac{dz-b}{-cz+a}. $$ By this, $$ (\mu\circ\nu)(z) = \frac{(ad-bc)z + (-ba+ab)}{(cd-dc)z + (-cb+da)} = z $$ so $\nu$ is an inverse of $\mu$ as required.
