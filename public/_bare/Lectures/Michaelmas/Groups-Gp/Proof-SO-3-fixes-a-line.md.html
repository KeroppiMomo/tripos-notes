$A$ is a product of at most 3 reflections, and since determinant of a reflection is -1, $A=I$ or $A=S_u S_v$ where $S_u\neq S_v$.

Since $n=3$, $P_u\inter P_v = l$ is a line, so $A$ fixes $l$.

To see that $\mrm{Fix}(A) = l$, if $x \not\in l$ but $S_uS_vx = x$, then $$ S_v x = S_u x \neq x. $$ But then $$ u \parallel S_u x - x = S_v x - x \parallel v $$ so $S_u \neq S_v$.
