Next, we will use the subgroup criterion.

First, $\mathrm{Isom}(X)\neq\emptyset$ since $\mathrm{Id}_X\in\mathrm{Isom}(X)$.

Next, For all $f,g\in\mathrm{Isom}(X)$ and for all $x,y\in X$, $$\begin{align*} \big|(f\circ g^{-1})(x) - (f\circ g^{-1})(y)\big| &= \big|f(g^{-1}(x)) - f(g^{-1}(y))\big| \\ &= |g^{-1}(x) - g^{-1}(y)| &&f \text{ is an isometry} \\ &= \big|g(g^{-1}(x)) - g(g^{-1}))(y)\big| &&g \text{ is an isometry} \\ &= |x - y|, \end{align*}$$ so $f\circ g^{-1} \in\mathrm{Isom}(X)$.
