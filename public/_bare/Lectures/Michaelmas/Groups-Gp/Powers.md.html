Notation (power) open_in_new

For any $a\in G$, denote

  • $a^0 = e$;

  • $a^n = a^{n-1}\cdot a$ for all $n=1,2,3,\ldots$;

  • $a^n = (a^{-1})^{-n}$ for all $n=-1,-2,-3,\ldots$.

Proposition (adding exponents) open_in_new

For any $a\in G$ and $m,n\in\mathbb Z$, $$ a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}. $$
