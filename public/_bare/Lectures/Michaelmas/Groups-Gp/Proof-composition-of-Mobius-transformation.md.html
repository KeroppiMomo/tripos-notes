This is just algebraic pain: $$\begin{align*} (\mu_1\circ\mu_2)(z) &= \mu_1\Big(\frac{a_2z + b_2}{c_2z + d_2}\Big) \\ &= \frac{a_1\Big(\frac{a_2z+b_2}{c_2z+d_2}\Big) +b_1}{c_1\Big(\frac{a_2z+b_2}{c_2z+d_2}\Big) + d_1} \\ &= \frac{a_1a_2z + a_1b_2 + b_1c_2 z + b_1d_2}{c_1a_2z+c_1b_2 + d_1c_2z + d_1d_2} \\ &= \frac{(a_1a_2 + b_1c_2)z + (a_1b_2 + b_1d_2)}{(c_1a_2+d_1c_2)z + (c_1b_2 + d_1d_2)} \end{align*}$$

Compare the result with $$ \begin{pmatrix} a_1 & b_1 \\ c_1 & d_1 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} a_2 & b_2 \\ c_2 & d_2 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}a_1a_2+b_1c_2 & a_1b_2+b_1d_2 \\ c_1a_2 + d_1c_2 & c_1b_2 + d_1d_2\end{pmatrix}.$$
