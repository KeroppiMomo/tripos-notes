Using the generating set of the Mobius group, it is sufficient to show that

  • $\alpha_a(z) = az$ for $a\in\C_\times$,

  • $\beta_b(z) = z+b$ for $b\in\C$, and

  • $\gamma(z) = 1/z$

send $C$ to a circle.

$\alpha_a$ and $\beta_b$ are trivial. For $\gamma$, suppose $C$ is an Euclidean circle with equation $$ |z-c| = r.$$ Then $\gamma(C)$ has equation $$ \Big|\frac1z - c\Big| = r. $$ This is equivalent to $$\begin{align*} \frac{1}{z\bar z} - \frac{c}{\bar z} - \frac{\bar c}z + c\bar c - r^2 &= 0 \\ (c\bar c - r^2)z\bar z - cz - \bar c\bar z + 1 &= 0. \end{align*}$$ If $c\bar c-r^2 = 0$, then $$\begin{align*} -cz-\bar c\bar z + 1 &= 0 \\ -\frac{z}{\bar c} - \frac{\bar z}{c} + \frac{1}{c\bar c} &= 0 \\ z\bar z-\frac{z}{\bar c} - \frac{\bar z}{c} + \frac{1}{c\bar c} &= z\bar z \\ \Big|z-\frac{1}{c}\Big|^2 &= |z|^2 \\ \Big|z-\frac1c\Big| &= |z| \end{align*}$$ which is an equation of a straight line. If $c\bar c-r^2 \neq 0$, then $$\begin{align*} z\bar z - \frac{c}{c\bar c-r^2}z - \frac{\bar c}{c\bar c-r^2}\bar z &= -\frac{1}{c\bar c-r^2} \\ \Big| z-\frac{\bar c}{c\bar c - r^2}\Big|^2 &= \frac{c\bar c}{(c\bar c - r^2)^2} - \frac{1}{c\bar c-r^2} \\ &= \frac{r^2}{(c\bar c-r^2)^2} >0 \end{align*}$$ which is an equation of a circle.

Now suppose $C$ is a line with equation $$ |z - a| = |z - b|. $$ Then $\gamma(C)$ has equation $$\begin{align*} \Big|\frac1z - a\Big| &= \Big|\frac1z - b\Big| \\ \frac{1}{z\bar z} - \frac{a}{\bar z} - \frac{\bar a}{z} + a\bar a &= \frac{1}{z\bar z} - \frac{b}{\bar z} - \frac{\bar b}{z} + b\bar b \\ (a\bar a-b\bar b)z\bar z - (a-b)z - (\bar a-\bar b)\bar z &= 0. \end{align*}$$ If $a\bar a - b\bar b = 0$, then $$\begin{align*} (a-b)z + (\bar a-\bar b)\bar z &= 0 \\ z\bar z + az + \bar a\bar z + a\bar a &= z\bar z + bz + \bar b\bar z + b\bar b \\ |z+\bar a| &= |z+\bar b|. \end{align*}$$ which is the equation of a straight line. If $a\bar a - b\bar b\neq 0$, then $$\begin{align*} z\bar z - \frac{a-b}{a\bar a - b\bar b} z - \frac{\bar a-\bar b}{a\bar a - b\bar b}\bar z &= 0 \\ \Big|z-\frac{\bar a-\bar b}{a\bar a - b\bar b}\Big|^2 &= \frac{(a-b)(\bar a-\bar b)}{(a\bar a - b\bar b)^2} \\ &= \frac{|a-b|^2}{(|a|^2 - |b|^2)^2} > 0 \end{align*}$$ which is the equation of a circle.
