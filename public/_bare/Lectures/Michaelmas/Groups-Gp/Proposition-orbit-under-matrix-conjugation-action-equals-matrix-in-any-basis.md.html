Consider the action $\gl n\R \actson M_n(\R)$ by matrix conjugation $$ (P, A) \mapsto PAP\inv. $$

Let $V$ be an $n$-dimensional vector space and let $\alpha: V\to V$ be a linear map. If $A \in M_n(\R)$ represents $\alpha$ in some basis, then the orbit $$ \gl n\R A = \sb{PAP\inv}{P\in\gl n\R} $$ equals the set of all matrices that represent $\alpha$ in any basis.
