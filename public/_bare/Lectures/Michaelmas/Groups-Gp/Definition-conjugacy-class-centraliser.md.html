• The orbit of $\gamma$ under the conjugation action is called the conjugacy class of $\gamma$, $$ \mrm{ccl}(\gamma) = \sb{g\gamma g^{-1}}{g\in G}. $$ This is the set of all conjugates of $\gamma$.

  • The stabiliser of $\gamma$ under the conjugation action is called the centraliser of $\gamma$, $$ C_G(\gamma) = \sb{g\in G}{g\gamma g^{-1} = \gamma}. $$ This is set of all elements in $G$ that commute with $\gamma$, i.e. $g\gamma = \gamma g$.
