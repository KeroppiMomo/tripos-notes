Clearly $\phi$ is a bijection.

Since $$ k + l = pn + (k +_n l)$$ where $p\in\mathbb Z$ and $+_n$ is the addition mod $n$ operation, we have $$\begin{align*} \phi(k+_n l) &= e^{2\pi i(k+_n l)/k} \\ &= e^{2\pi i(pn)/n} e^{2\pi i(k+l)/n} \\ &= (1) e^{2\pi ik/n} e^{2\pi il/n} \\ &= \phi(k)\phi(l) \end{align*}$$ so $\phi$ is a homomorphism.
