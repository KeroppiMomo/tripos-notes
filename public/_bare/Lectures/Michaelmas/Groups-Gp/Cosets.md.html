Definition (right coset) open_in_new

Let $H\leq G$ and $\gamma\in G$. The subset $$ H\gamma = \sb{h\gamma}{h\in H} $$ is called a right coset of $H$ in $G$.

The set of right cosets of $H$ in $G$ is denoted as $H\setminus G$.

Proposition (right cosets partition) open_in_new

For any subgroup $H\leq G$, the right cosets $H\setminus G$ partition $G$.

Proof: right cosets are orbits and orbits partition.

Definition (left coset) open_in_new

Let $H\leq G$ and $\gamma\in G$. The subset $$\gamma H = \sb{\gamma h}{h\in H}$$ is called a left coset of $H$ in $G$.

The notation $G/H$ denotes the set of all left cosets of $H$ in $G$.

Proposition (left coset as orbit of action) open_in_new
