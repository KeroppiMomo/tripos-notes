Firstly $S_v(x) = x - 2(x\cdot v)v$ is linear in $x$ so $S_v \in M_n(\R)$.

Since $S_v^2 = I$, $S_v$ is invertible, so $S_v \in \gl n\R$.

Finally, for all $x\in\R^n$, $$\begin{align*} \|S_v(x)\|^2 &= \big(x-2(x\cdot v)v\big) \cdot\big(x-2(x\cdot v)v\big) \\ &= \|x\|^2 - 4(x\cdot v)(x\cdot v) + 4(x\cdot v)(x\cdot v)(v\cdot v) \\ &= \|x\|^2, \end{align*}$$ so $S_v \in O(n)$ as required.
