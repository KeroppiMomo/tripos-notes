Let $x_1, x_2, y_1, y_2\in\mathbb C$. If $$\begin{align*} |y_1 - x_1| &= |y_1 - x_2| \\ |y_2 - x_1| &= |y_2 - x_2|, \end{align*}$$ then $x_2-x_1$ is perpendicular to $y_2-y_1$.

Proof (kite lemma) open_in_new

Using geometry

$\Delta x_1 x_2 y_1\cong\Delta x_1x_2y_2$ by SSS, and so $\angle y_1 x_1 x_2 = \angle y_2x_1x_2$. Since $|x_1-y_2| = |x_1-y_1|$, we have $x_1x_2\perp y_1y_2$.
