Firstly this is an action because $$ e(\gamma) = \gamma e^{-1} = \gamma e = \gamma $$ and $$ (hh')(\gamma) = \gamma(hh')^{-1} = (\gamma h'^{-1})h^{-1} = (h'(\gamma))h^{-1} = h(h'(\gamma)). $$

Now an orbit of $\gamma\in G$ under this action is $$ \sb{\gamma h^{-1}}{h\in H}, $$ which is the same as $$ \sb{\gamma h}{h\in H}. $$
