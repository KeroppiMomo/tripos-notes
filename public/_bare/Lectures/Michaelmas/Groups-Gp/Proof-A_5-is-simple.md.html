Suppose $N\lhd A_5$. We want to show that $N = 1$ or $A_5$.

$N$ is a union of conjugacy classes of $A_5$. With reference to the sizes of conjugacy classes of $A_5$, since $e\in N$, the possible order of $N$ are:

Since $|N||A_5:N| = |A_5|$ by Lagrange's theorem, $|N| = 1$ or 60, so $N = 1$ or $A_5$ as required.
