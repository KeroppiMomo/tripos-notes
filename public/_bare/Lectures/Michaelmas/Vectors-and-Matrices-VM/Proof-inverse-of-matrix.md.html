Combining the results of expanding along a row correctly and wrongly, using summation notation, $$\begin{align*} A_{ik} \Delta_{jk} &= (\det A)\delta_{ij}, \\ A_{ki} \Delta_{kj} &= (\det A)\delta_{ij}. \end{align*}$$

Therefore, for matrix $B$ defined as $$ B_{ij} = \frac{\Delta_{ji}}{\det A}, $$ the product $AB$ is $$\begin{align*} (AB)_{ij} &= A_{ik} B_{kj} \\ &= \frac{A_{ik}\Delta_{jk}}{\det A} \\ &= \delta_{ij} \end{align*}$$ so $AB = I$.
