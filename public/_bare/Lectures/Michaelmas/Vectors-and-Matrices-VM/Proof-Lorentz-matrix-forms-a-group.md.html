• Closure: for any $v_1 = \tanh\alpha_1, v_2 = \tanh\alpha_2$, $$\begin{align*} B_{v_1} B_{v_2} &= \pmat{\cosh\alpha_1 & \sinh\alpha_1 \\ \sinh\alpha_1 & \cosh\alpha_1}\pmat{\cosh\alpha_2 & \sinh\alpha_2 \\ \sinh\alpha_2 & \cosh\alpha_2} \\ &= \pmat{\cosh(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2) & \sinh(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2) \\ \cosh(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2) & \sinh(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2)} \\ &= B_{v_3} \end{align*}$$ where $$ v_3 = \tanh(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2) = \frac{v_1 + v_2}{1 + v_1v_2} $$

  • Associativity follows from matrix multiplication.

  • Identity: $$ B_0 = \pmat{1&0 \\ 0&1}. $$

  • Inverse: $B_v\inv = B_{-v}$.
