$$\begin{align*} |z_1 + z_2|^2 &= (z_1 + z_2)\overline{(z_1 + z_2)} \\ &= z_1\overline{z_1} + z_2\overline{z_2} + z_1\overline{z_2} + \overline{z_1}z_2 \\ &= |z_1|^2 + |z_2|^2 + z_1\overline{z_2} + \overline{z_1\overline{z_2}} \\ &= |z_1|^2 + |z_2|^2 + \color{yellow} 2\,\text{Re}(z_1\overline{z_2}) \\ &\leq |z_1|^2 + |z_2|^2 + \color{yellow} 2|z_1\overline{z_2}| \\ &= |z_1|^2 + |z_2|^2 + 2|z_1||\overline{z_2}| \\ &= |z_1|^2 + |z_2|^2 + 2|z_1||z_2| \\ &= (|z_1| + |z_2|)^2 \end{align*}$$ Since $|z_1+z_2|$ and $|z_1|+|z_2|$ are both positive, $$ |z_1+|z_2| \leq |z_1| + |z_2| $$ as required.
