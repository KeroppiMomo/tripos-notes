Define $$\begin{align*} \vec x + \vec y &= (x_1+y_1, x_2+y_2, \ldots, x_n+y_n) \\ \vec 0 &= (0,0,\ldots,0) \\ \lambda\vec x &= (\lambda x_1,\lambda x_2,\ldots,\lambda x_n) \\ -\vec x &= (-x_1,-x_2,\ldots,-x_n) \end{align*}$$

It can easily be checked that all conditions for a vector space are satisfied.
