We can decompose $A$ into a symmetric part $S$ and an antisymmetric part $B$ $$ A = S + B. $$

For the antisymmetric part, note that $$\begin{align*} \v x^T B \v x &= (\v x^T B \v x)^T \\ &= \v x^T B^T \v x \\ &= -\v x^T B \v x \end{align*}$$ so $$ \v x^T B \v x = 0. $$

Hence $$ \v x^T A\v x = \v x^T S\v x. $$
