Given function $f(x,y)$, the partial derivative of $f$ with respect to $x$ is $$ \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Big|_y = \lim_{\delta x\to 0} \frac{f(x+\delta x, y) - f(x,y)}{\delta x} $$ and that with respect to $y$ is $$ \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Big|_x = \lim_{\delta y\to 0} \frac{f(x, y+\delta y) - f(x,y)}{\delta y}. $$
