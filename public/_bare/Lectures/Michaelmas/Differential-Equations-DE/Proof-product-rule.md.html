Method 1: using limits

$$\begin{align*} h'(x) &= \lim_{h\to 0}\frac1h\big(u(x+h)v(x+h) - u(x)v(x)\big) \\ &= \lim_{h\to 0}\frac1h\big(u(x+h)v(x+h) -u(x+h)v(x) + u(x+h)v(x) - u(x)v(x)\big) \\ &= \lim_{h\to 0}u(x+h)\cdot\frac1h\big(v(x+h)-v(x)\big) + \lim_{h\to0} v(x)\cdot\frac1h\big(u(x+h) - u(x)\big) \\ &= u(x)v'(x) + u'(x)v(x) \end{align*}$$
