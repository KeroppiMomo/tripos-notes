For a family of functions $f(x;\lambda)$ where the parameter $\lambda$ lables the different members of the family, define $$ I(\lambda) = \int_{a(\lambda)}^{b(\lambda)} f(x;\lambda)\,dx. $$

Then $$ \frac{dI}{d\lambda} = \int_{a(\lambda)}^{b(\lambda)}\frac{\partial f}{\partial \lambda}(x,\lambda)\,dx + \frac{db}{d\lambda}f\big(b(\lambda);\lambda\big) - \frac{da}{d\lambda}f\big(a(\lambda);\lambda\big).$$
