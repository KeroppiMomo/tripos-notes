A system of first-order linear ODEs has a phase portrait with points $$ \v Y = \pmat{y_1 \\ y_2 \\ \vdots \\ y_n}. $$

For the equation $\dv Y = M\v Y$, consider the non-degenerate general solution $$ \v Y(t) = \v v_1 e^{\lambda_1 t} + \v v_2 e^{\lambda_2 t}, \quad 0\neq \lambda_1 \neq \lambda_2 \neq 0. $$

$\v 0$ is a fixed point. The phase portraits depend on $\lambda_1, \lambda_2$:
