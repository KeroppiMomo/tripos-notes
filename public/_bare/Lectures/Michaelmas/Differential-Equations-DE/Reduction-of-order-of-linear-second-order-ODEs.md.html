General form $$ y'' + p(x) y' + q(x)y = 0. $$

This is hard to solve, but if we have one solution $y_1(x)$, we can find a second one $y_2(x)$ by reduction of order.

This can also be done with Abel's theorem. See that for formula.

Derivation

This generalises to any order.
