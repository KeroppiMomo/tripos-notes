Let $\delta x = x(t+\delta t)-x(t), \delta y = y(t+\delta y)-y(t),$ and $\delta f=f(x+\delta x,y+\delta y) - f(x,y)$.

Then $$\begin{align*} \delta f &= f(x+\delta x,y+\delta y) - f(x+\delta x,y) + f(x+\delta x,y) - f(x,y) \\ &= f_y(x+\delta x, y)\,\delta y + o(\delta y) + f_x(x,y)\,\delta x + o(\delta x) \\ &= \big(f_y(x,y) + f_{yx}(x,y)\,\delta x + o(\delta x)\big)\,\delta y + f_x(x,y) \delta x + o(\delta x) \\ \lim_{\delta t\to 0}\frac{\delta f}{\delta t} &= \lim_{\delta t\to 0}\Big[ \big(f_y(x,y) + f_{yx}(x,y)\,\delta x + o(\delta x)\big)\frac{\delta y}{\delta t} + f_x(x,y)\frac{\delta x}{\delta t} + \frac{o(\delta x)}{\delta t}\Big] \\ &= f_y(x,y)\frac{dy}{dt} + f_x(x,y)\frac{\delta x}{\delta t} \end{align*}$$ as required.
