The Wronskian of $n$ functions $y_i(x)$ for $i=1,2,\ldots, n$ is the determinant of the fundamental matrix, defined by $$ W(x) = \begin{vmatrix} y_1 & y_2 & \ldots & y_n \\ y_1' & y_2' & \ldots & y_n' \\ y_1'' & y_2'' & \ldots & y_n'' \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ y_1^{(n-1)} & y_2^{(n-1)} & \ldots & y_n^{(n-1)} \end{vmatrix}. $$
