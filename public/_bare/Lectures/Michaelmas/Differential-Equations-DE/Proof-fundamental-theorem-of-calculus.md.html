$$\begin{align*} \frac{dF}{dx} &= \lim_{h\to0}\frac1h(F(x+h) - F(x)) \\ &= \lim_{h\to0}\frac1h\Big(\int_a^{x+h}f(t)\,dt - \int_a^x f(t)\,dt\Big) \\ &= \lim_{h\to0}\frac1h\int_x^{x+h}f(t)\,dt \\ &= \lim_{h\to0}\frac1h\big(f(c)h\big) &&\text{mean value theorem, }c\in[x,x+h] \\ &= \lim_{h\to0}f(c) \\ &= f(x) \end{align*}$$
