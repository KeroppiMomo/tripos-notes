Consider the general equation $$ a(x) y' + b(x) y = c(x). $$ We usually write it in the standard form by dividing $a(x)$: $$ y' + p(x) y = f(x). $$

We let the integrating factor be $$ \mu(x) = \exp\Big(\int^x p(u)\,du\Big) $$ and our solution is $$ y(x) = \frac{1}{\mu(x)}\int^x \mu(u)f(u)\,du. $$
