We usually omit the evaluation line and write $$\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Big|_y \equiv \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \equiv f_x.$$

Mixed partial differentiation is written as $$\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\Big(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Big) \equiv \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y} \equiv f_{yx}.$$
