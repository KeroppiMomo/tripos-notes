Definition (definite integral) open_in_new

The integral of a function $f(x)$ is defined as $$ \int_a^b f(x)\,dx = \lim_{N\to\infty}\sum_{n=0}^{N-1}f(x_n)\Delta x = \lim_{x_0\to 0}\sum_{n=0}^{N-1}f(x_n)\Delta x $$ where $\Delta x = (b-a)/N$ and $x_n = a + n\Delta x$.
