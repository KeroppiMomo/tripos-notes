Define $$ \frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\Big|_z $$ be the partial derivative of $y$ with respect to $x$, holding $z$ fixed. This means that $$ \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\Big|_z = 0. $$

Proposition (partial derivative of implicit equation) open_in_new

For a general implicit equation $f(x,y,z) = c$, $$\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\Big|_z = -\frac{\partial f/\partial x\big|_{y,z}}{\partial f/\partial y\big|_{x,z}}.$$
