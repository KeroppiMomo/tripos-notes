By the binomial theorem, $$\begin{align*} \lim_{k\to\infty}\Big(1+\frac xk\Big)^k &= \lim_{k\to\infty}\sum_{n=0}^k \frac{k(k-1)\ldots (k-n)}{n!}\Big(\frac xk\Big)^n \\ &= \lim_{k\to\infty}\sum_{n=0}^k \frac{k}{k}\frac{k-1}{k}\ldots \frac{k-n}{k}\frac{x^n}{n!} \\ &= \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!}. \end{align*}$$
